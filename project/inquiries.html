Frequently Asked Questions

General Information FAQs

What is Toolboxify?

Toolboxify is a tool management program that allows tradesmen to easily track and manage their tool inventory without the hassle of other programs, allowing you to focus on what really matters.


How can I sign Up?

Sign up for Toolboxify's testing program here or by visiting Toolboxify.app/inolved and filling out the provided digital form with required information. Our team will review your application and reach out to you regarding your application status and eligibility.


Is there a free trial available?

You may be eligible for a free trial through our testing program. Future trials and discounts may be available.


Where can I learn more about Toolboxify's features?

You can explore more detailed information about Toolboxify on our information page or by visiting Toolboxify.app/information.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. No cancellation fees apply.

Technical FAQs

What devices are compatible?

Toolboxify is a cross-platform solution that we are working on integrating into iOS, Android, and web-browser capable devices.


Do I need to install any software?

Toolboxify is entirely cloud-based and accessible through your browser. For iOS and Android users, you can find Toolboxify on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.


How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking "Forgot Password" on the login page and following the instructions.


What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

Please submit a support ticket or email our help desk.

Privacy and Security FAQs

How is my data protected?

We are committed to maintaining the integrity of your data's security. We use encryption and secure servers to protect your data. Please see our Privacy Policy for details.

Do you share my personal information with third parties?

No, we value your privacy and do not share your information without your consent.


What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data will be stored for a duration after cancellation, after which it may be permanently deleted.
