Toolboxify News

Development Update: November 2024 - More UI, Administrators

We've been hard at work refining Toolboxify, and we're excited to share the progress we made during this last sprint! Here's what's new:

  1. A Fresh New Look
    We've completely overhauled the user interface (UI) to make Toolboxify easier to use and navigate. The new design is clean, intuitive, and user-friendly—no technical know-how required!
  2. Navigable UI
    You can now move seamlessly through the program. We've added menus and pages that are simple to access and designed to help you find what you need quickly.
  3. GUI Account Creation
    Creating an account is now as simple as filling out a quick form. No complicated steps—just a straightforward process to get you started.
  4. Inventory Management
    Managing your tools just got easier. You can now:
    • Add tools to your inventory with a few clicks.
    • Remove tools that you no longer need.
  5. Inventory Tracking
    Stay on top of your tools with basic inventory tracking. A simple, clear visual lets you see what tools you have on hand at a glance.
  6. Tool Check-In/Check-Out
    We've introduced a new feature for tool check-in and check-out! This makes it easier to keep track of which tools are currently in use and who has them.

These updates are all about making Toolboxify more practical, efficient, and user-friendly for tradespeople and businesses. We're committed to building a tool management platform that works for you, and we're just getting started.

Have questions or feedback? We'd love to hear from you—your input helps us make Toolboxify even better!

Stay tuned for more updates in our next sprint!

Development Update: October 2024 - Admin Tools & Enhanced UX

In this sprint, we've taken significant steps to improve the program's usability and add functionality for administrators. Here's what's new:

  1. Administrator-Specific Menu
    We've created a separate menu exclusively for administrators. This menu allows admins to easily access all of their admin-specific tasks, making management tasks quicker and more organized.
  2. Edit Personal Information
    Users can now edit their personal information directly within the program. Whether it's updating an email address, phone number, or other details, this functionality provides more control over your profile.
  3. Improved UI and UX
    The user interface is now even easier to navigate, with better menu organization and clearer labels. We've also made the overall user experience (UX) smoother, helping you get things done more efficiently with fewer clicks and less confusion.

These updates are aimed at making Toolboxify even more accessible and effective for all users, while empowering administrators with the tools they need to manage their work effortlessly.

As always, we value your feedback! Let us know what you think about these changes, and stay tuned for more improvements in the next sprint.
